Activity Tracker Configuration

Configure your activity tracker settings below:

Below are the explanations for the settings you can configure in the activity tracker.

  • Screenshot Interval: Set how often screenshots are taken (in seconds).
  • Blur Screenshots: Choose whether to apply a blur effect on captured screenshots.
  • Scripted Activity Threshold: Define the threshold for detecting scripted mouse movements.
  • Low Battery Threshold: Set the battery percentage below which tracking will pause.

Use this form to ensure your activity tracker is configured to your preferences.
